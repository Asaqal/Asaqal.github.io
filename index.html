Lights Out!

Moves: 0

Time: 00:00

Ahmad Saqal

In Lights Out!, the goal is to make the entire board the same color.

This is done by clicking on a light. In a plus shape on the chosen light, the surrounding lights will be flipped

(ex: lights already on turn off, and lights already off turn on).

At the bottom you can change the theme, reset the current board, or generate a new board.
